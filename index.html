  • Healthcare for All

    Uniquely positioned, UFCH provides high quality medical services to vulnerable populations in need through its Foundation Clinic, Wheels for Life Community Health Van, Specialty, Surgical and Mental Health departments.

    Learn More

  • Remote Diagnosis and Training Program

    Thanks to a multi-year grant from CISCO, UFCH can provide remote diagnosis to orphans and children from poor families in Sichuan Province. With hospital partners in Chengdu and Beijing, expert evaluation is made remotely and a plan for treatment is scheduled.

    Learn More

  • United Family-Marbury Program for Youth Rehabilitation

    Collaboration between basketball star Stephon Marbury and the United Family Charitable Fund created the ‘Youth Rehabilitation Program’ for underserved families and children.

    Learn More

  • China Children’s Medical Care Program

    Since 2001, UFCH has provided hundreds of orphans and children from poor families with the medical care, inpatient care, surgical care, nutrition evaluation and vaccinations thanks to a large annual in-kind donation from United Family Healthcare.

    Learn More
